Our Story

Fitvate was founded in 2014 with a simple vision: to create a fitness center that feels like home. What started as a small studio has grown into a state-of-the-art facility serving thousands of members.

Our founders, passionate fitness enthusiasts themselves, understood that the journey to fitness is unique for everyone. That's why we've built a space that accommodates all fitness levels, from beginners taking their first steps to athletes pushing their limits.

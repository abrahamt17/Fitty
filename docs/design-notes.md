# Design Notes

## Current direction

- dark premium fitness look
- warm orange accent with cool blue support tones
- rounded cards and glassy panels
- bold display headings with cleaner body text

## Content tone

- premium but not luxury-for-no-reason
- coaching-led instead of generic gym copy
- clear outcomes over vague hype

## Rule of thumb

When adding new sections, match the current tone before adding extra effects.
